# About

Co01ley Studios focuses on creating Roblox games and atmospheric audio.  
The studio aims to build games that mainly involve progression, structure, and a distinct identity.

## What We Work On

- Archive‑style RBXL loading systems.
- Ambient and conceptual music.
- Varied worldbuilding and progression concepts.
