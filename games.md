# 🎮 Our Games

## CMToH — Co01ley's Mini Towers of Hell
A story‑driven EToH fangame featuring:
- 1 current world  
- 3 additional worlds planned  
- A dedicated development team  

---

## EToH 2.5xs
A compact reinterpretation of classic EToH.  
Towers, steeples, citadels, and obelisks are reimagined as:

- **Floors**  
- **Rooms**  
- **Edifices**  
- **Steeples** (compressed obelisks)

This creates a faster, smaller, progression‑focused experience.

---

## Archive Game (Temporary Name)
A community‑powered project where players can submit `.rbxl` files to be curated and published as individual places.

Players can:
- Submit RBXL creations  
- Have them reviewed and added if accepted  
- Explore a growing library of community‑made games  
- Enter each place through portals in the hub  

This lets users publish games on Roblox without age verification.
